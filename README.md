Flipkart Mobile Data Analysis & Visualization Dashboard
Project Overview

This project presents an interactive sales and product analytics dashboard built in Microsoft Excel using Flipkart mobile dataset. The dashboard provides insights into brand performance, ratings, discounts, pricing distribution, and customer engagement through reviews and ratings.

The objective is to analyze mobile product data and extract business insights that support pricing strategy, brand positioning, and sales performance evaluation.

Tools & Technologies

Microsoft Excel

Pivot Tables and Pivot Charts

Slicers for dynamic filtering

Data cleaning and transformation

Dashboard design techniques

Dataset Summary

The dataset includes mobile product information such as:

Brand name

Mobile model

MRP and selling price (MSP)

Discount value

Customer ratings

Number of ratings

Number of reviews

Price range categories

Dashboard Insights
Brand & Rating Analysis

Brand-wise rating comparison shows performance differences across major brands such as Apple, Samsung, Vivo, Realme, and others.

Apple and Samsung maintain higher average rating consistency.

Ratings Volume Analysis

Apple and POCO show higher rating counts, indicating stronger customer engagement.

High rating volume often correlates with higher product visibility.

Review Analysis

Top mobiles based on review count highlight high-demand products.

Certain Redmi and POCO models dominate in review numbers.

Discount Analysis

Samsung models appear frequently among top discounted mobiles.

Higher discounts are observed in mid-range and premium segments.

Price Range Distribution

Majority of mobiles fall under the “Below 20K” price segment.

Fewer models are positioned above 40K, indicating market concentration in budget and mid-range segments.

Recruitment of Popular Models

Top 5 mobiles by ratings and discounts help identify best-performing SKUs.

Premium variants often receive strong ratings but moderate discounting.

Interactive Features

The dashboard includes dynamic slicers for:

Brand

Mobile Name

Users can filter data to analyze specific brands or individual models instantly.
